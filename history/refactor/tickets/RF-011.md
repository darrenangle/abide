# RF-011: Harden Ghazal Canonical Pass/Fail

- Priority: `P1`
- Status: `done`
- Area: `form verification consistency`
- Origin: `FOLLOWUP-003`

## Problem

`Ghazal` still uses bespoke score/pass logic that can report `passed=True` for a structurally plausible poem with a broken later radif/qafiya line. That is why `Ghazal` had to be excluded from the conservative `training_safe` subset in RF-010.

## Primary Files

- `src/abide/forms/ghazal.py`
- `tests/forms/test_training_safe_forms.py`
- `tests/fixtures/test_form_validation.py`
- `src/abide/forms/catalog.py`

## Reproduction

- Mutating one later rhyming line in `GHAZAL_SYNTHETIC_PERFECT` to remove the shared `radif` still yields `score=0.8333` and `passed=True`.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Canonical `passed` for `Ghazal` is gated on the defining radif/qafiya requirements rather than the blended mean score alone.
- Regressions cover the broken-radif near miss directly.
- Reevaluate whether `Ghazal` belongs in the `training_safe` subset after the fix; promote it only if the new adversarial harness passes.
