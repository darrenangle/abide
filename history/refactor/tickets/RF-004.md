# RF-004: Align Blank Verse And Related Metrical Form Claims

- Priority: `P1`
- Status: `done`
- Area: `form fidelity`
- Origin: `AUDIT-002`

## Problem

`BlankVerse` and several related metrical forms claim unrhymed or meter-specific behavior while mostly checking syllable counts.

## Primary Files

- `src/abide/forms/blank_verse.py`
- `src/abide/forms/couplet.py`
- `src/abide/forms/quatrain.py`
- `src/abide/forms/ottava_rima.py`

## Acceptance Criteria

- Either enforce the claimed property or rewrite descriptions/instructions to match implementation.
- `BlankVerse` no longer passes obviously rhymed text as canonical blank verse.

## Resolution

- Added `EndRhymeDensity` and required `BlankVerse` to pass that unrhymed check even in lenient mode.
- Narrowed meter-heavy descriptions in blank verse, couplets, quatrains, ottava-rima variants, and mini-eval prompts to match syllable-based verification.
- Added regression tests for rhymed blank verse false positives and the new unrhymed constraint.
